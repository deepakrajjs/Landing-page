Our Story

Founded in 2015, Paws & Tails started with a simple mission: to provide dog owners with high-quality products that promote the health and happiness of their furry companions.

We carefully select and test every product in our inventory to ensure it meets our strict quality standards. All our products are sustainably sourced and made with pet-safe materials.
